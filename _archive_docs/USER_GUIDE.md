## Getting Started

### Creating an Account
1. Visit the website and click on the "Register" or "Sign Up" button
2. Fill in the required information:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Desired username
   - Password (must meet security requirements)
3. Click "Create Account"
4. Check your email for a verification link (if required)
5. Once verified, you can sign in with your credentials

### Signing In
1. Click on "Login" or "Sign In"
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard/profile page

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Account Issues
**Problem**: Cannot log in
**Solutions**:
- Check your email and password for typos
- Reset your password if needed
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser

**Problem**: Forgot password
**Solution**:
1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a password reset link
4. Follow the instructions to reset your password

**Problem**: Account locked
**Solution**: Contact support if you believe your account was locked in error

#### Shopping and Ordering
**Problem**: Items disappearing from cart
**Solutions**:
- Sign in to create a persistent cart
- Check if you're using private browsing mode
- Clear browser cache and cookies

**Problem**: Payment issues
**Solutions**:
- Verify your payment information is correct
- Try a different payment method
- Contact your bank to ensure international transactions are enabled
- Try again later if the error is temporary

**Problem**: Order not showing in history
**Solutions**:
- Refresh the page
- Check if you're signed into the correct account
- Wait a few minutes and check again (sometimes there's a slight delay)

#### Product Issues
**Problem**: Product images not loading
**Solutions**:
- Check your internet connection
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

**Problem**: Search not returning results
**Solutions**:
- Try different search terms
- Use the advanced search filters
- Contact support if the issue persists

#### Performance Issues
**Problem**: Slow loading times
**Solutions**:
- Check your internet connection speed
- Close other browser tabs/applications
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is my personal information secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information. All data is transmitted securely using HTTPS, and passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing.

**Q: Can I change my account information?**
A: Yes, you can update your personal information, email address, and password in the "Profile" section of your account.

**Q: How do I return a product?**
A: Our return policy allows returns within 30 days of purchase. Products must be in original condition with tags attached. Visit the "Returns" section in your account or contact customer support for more information.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
A: Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Check out our shipping policy page for more details.

### Account Questions

**Q: How do I update my email address?**
A: Go to your account profile and click on "Edit Profile". You can update your email address there, and you'll need to verify the new email address.

**Q: How do I close my account?**
A: Contact customer support to request account closure. Note that this action is irreversible and will delete all your order history.

**Q: Why was I logged out automatically?**
A: For security purposes, accounts are automatically logged out after a period of inactivity. This helps protect your account from unauthorized access.

### Order Questions

**Q: How long does shipping take?**
A: Shipping times depend on your location and selected shipping method. Standard shipping typically takes 3-7 business days, while express shipping takes 1-2 business days.

**Q: How can I track my order?**
A: Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can also view your order status and tracking information in your account.

**Q: Can I change my order after placing it?**
A: For minor changes (like shipping address), contact customer support immediately. Once an order has been processed and shipped, changes may not be possible.

**Q: What if my order arrives damaged?**
A: Please contact customer support immediately with photos of the damage. We'll work with you to resolve the issue with a replacement or refund.

### Payment Questions

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash on delivery (where available).

**Q: Is there an extra charge for using a credit card?**
A: No, we don't charge any additional fees for using a credit card.

**Q: When will I be charged?**
A: You'll be charged immediately when you place your order. If the order is cancelled, the charge will be refunded to your original payment method.

### Product Questions

**Q: How do I know if a product is in stock?**
A: Product pages display the current stock status. If a product is out of stock, it will be marked as "Out of Stock" and the "Add to Cart" button will be disabled.

**Q: Can I order a product that's out of stock?**
A: Some out-of-stock products offer a "Notify Me When Available" option. Otherwise, you'll need to wait until the product is restocked.

**Q: What is your refund policy?**
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. Items must be in original condition with all tags attached. Custom items may have different policies.

**Q: Are there taxes on my purchase?**
A: Applicable taxes will be calculated and displayed during checkout based on your shipping address.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: Our platform works best with Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions). Older browsers may have limited functionality.

**Q: Can I use the website in my preferred language?**
A: Yes, our platform supports multiple languages. Use the language selector in the top navigation bar to switch between supported languages (English, Persian, Arabic).

**Q: Is the website accessible for users with disabilities?**
A: We strive to meet accessibility standards. Our site includes keyboard navigation support, screen reader compatibility, and adjustable text sizes. Contact us if you have specific accessibility needs.

**Q: How do I clear my browser cache?**
A: The process varies by browser:
- Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data
- Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data
- Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data → Remove All
- Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Choose what to clear
